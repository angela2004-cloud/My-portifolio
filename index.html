02 / About

Who I Am

I’m Umubyeyi Angel, a Year II Computer Science student in the FEB Day Program at Institut d’Enseignement Supérieur de Ruhengeri (INES-Ruhengeri), one of Rwanda’s leading technology institutions.

Growing up in Musanze, I became fascinated by how technology can solve real problems for real communities. This passion led me to pursue Computer Science, where I discovered a deep love for frontend development and building interfaces that are not only functional but beautifully crafted.

My career goal is to become a full-stack developer working on impactful digital products that serve the Rwandan community and beyond — particularly in education, health, and public services.
